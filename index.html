Layer8 Communication Tool

CSUN's computer security club, Layer 8, educates students about information security and allows students to practice their skills on the latest technological advancements in software and hardware. Members of Layer 8 engage in inciteful presentations and labs during weekly meetings and participate in exciting competitive events held throughout the year such as capture the flag (CTF) events, seasonal National Cyber League (NCL) events, and the yearly Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (CCDC).




Our Vision

       
  • Our Vision We are committed to creating an educational environment for students to learn about the field of Information Security, become hands-on in labs to develop technical skills, learn best practices and ethics within the cyber world, and provide a place to gather to discuss all things cyber within the community of CalState Northridge.



Our Mission

       
  • Our mission is to cultivate a community of students passionate about cybersecurity by providing hands-on learning experiences, insightful industry lectures, and engaging Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions. We strive to empower our members with the practical skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the cybersecurity field, fostering an environment of collaboration, innovation, and professional growth.
